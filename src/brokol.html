how we got here

We started Brokol in November 2022 as a food waste app and spent a full year trying to earn habits through inventory tracking, reminders, and smart lists. It helped some households, but the people who kept showing up were ADHDers bending the product into a coping system.

So we stopped guessing and spent the first half of 2024 listening. More than 200 conversations with ADHDers, nutritionists, coaches, and anyone who would trade stories taught us that food struggles were just one symptom of a larger education gap.

That insight led to a lean meal planner sprint that ran for five to six months. We charged for early prototypes, kept iterating with the same users, and still heard the same refrain: "teach us first." In mid-2025 we dropped the remaining app surfaces and committed to The ADHD Weasel newsletter.
